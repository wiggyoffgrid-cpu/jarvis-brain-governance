## Card Numbering

XML cards within domains do NOT use D-series numbers. Cards use their own naming conventions per domain:

- People cards: person_name.xml (e.g., eric_legault.xml)
- Client cards: person_name.xml within company/wiggy_contacts/ folder
- Equipment XMLs: equipment_name_partN.xml
- Future domains define their own card naming in their doctrine page

---

## Version Format

- Version suffix: v1.0, v2.0, v3.1, etc.
- No _FROM_Rev lineage in filenames
- No LOCKED in filenames (use FINAL if needed)
- Highest version number is the canonical version
